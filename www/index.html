Our Philosophy

Crafted for those who savour the extraordinary

At Aurum, we believe that dining is an art form — a choreography of flavours, textures, and aromas drawn from the finest culinary traditions across the globe. Every dish is a love letter to the ingredients that inspire us.

Our multi-cuisine menu traverses continents — from the delicate umami of Japan to the bold spices of the Levant — unified by our commitment to uncompromising quality and seasonal provenance.

The Artisan

Chef Arjun Mehra

"I don't cook food. I compose experiences — each plate a movement in an unwritten symphony."

With over 18 years across Michelin-starred kitchens in Paris, Tokyo, and New York, Chef Arjun returned home to Chennai with a singular vision: to honour global technique while celebrating the soul of Indian ingredients.

His 12-course tasting menu at Aurum has been lauded as one of the most inventive dining experiences in South Asia.
